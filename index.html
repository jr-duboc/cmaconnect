In debt? Need help?

CMA Connect Fareham exists to help anyone worried about money or debt to access free, confidential debt advice.

  • Call 077777777,
  • Come to St John's Church hall (5A Upper St Michael's Grove, Fareham PO14 1DN) any Saturday 10am-12pm to make an appointment,
  • or email us at contact@cmsfareham.co.uk

How can we help you?

Our befrienders can help and support anyone worried about debt to access FREE advice, whatever your circumstances, background or faith.
Please don't be afraid to ask for help. Whatever your problem, however big or small, it always seems so much better when you share it with someone.

Budget

We can help you work out a plan of how you could manage your money each month.

Access to free debt advice

We can help you access free, confidential debt advice from a national charity.

Contacting people you owe money to

We can support you as you contact people you owe money to.

A listening ear

We are here to listen and talk through your worries with you. Our befrienders will also be happy to pray with you if you would like that.

Why are we here?

Because we're a group of Christians who believe, even in the most difficult of times, there is hope for the future.
