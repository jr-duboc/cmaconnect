How can we help you?

Our befrienders are here to help and support anyone worried about debt and provide access to FREE advice, whatever your circumstances, background or faith.
Please don't be afraid to ask for help. Whatever your worry or concern, big or small, we are here to listen to you.

Budget

We can help you plan how you can have a manageable budget for your money each month.

Access to free debt advice

CMA Connect Fareham are part of a national charity offering access to free, confidential debt advice.

Contacting people you owe money to

We can support you, and show how to contact the people you owe money to.

A listening ear

We are here to listen and talk through your worries with you. Our befrienders believe prayer changes situations, and are able to pray for and with you if you would like that.

Why are we here?

Because we're a group of Christians who believe, even in the most difficult of times, there is hope for the future.
